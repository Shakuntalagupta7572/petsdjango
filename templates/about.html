Pet Wellness Tracker

A pet wellness tracker is a device or application designed to monitor various aspects of a pet's health and well-being. Here are multiple points to consider about pet wellness trackers

Activity Monitoring:

This can help you ensure they're getting enough exercise and detect any changes in their activity levels, which could indicate health issues.

11. Emergency Assistance Module

The Emergency Assistance Module provides users with quick access to emergency resources and contacts in case of pet-related emergencies. It includes information on nearby veterinary clinics, animal hospitals, pet emergency hotlines, and first aid instructions for common emergencies.

Localization and Internationalization

It allows users to choose their preferred language, date/time formats, and measurement units for a personalized user experience. It ensures accessibility and inclusivity for users worldwide.

Medication Management

It provides reminders for administering medications and tracks medication history for each pet.
